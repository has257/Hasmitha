Additional Information

Elements of House Wiring:

  • Fuses and circuit breakers,Electric switch,Main switch,Plug,Socket outlet,Lamp holder,Incandescing Light

House Wiring Tools:

  • Combination pliers are used for holding, twisting, or cutting wires. A double blade electric knife has two blades: one for removing insulation from wires and another for cleaning the wires. A screwdriver is used for loosening, tightening, and keeping screws in position. A soldering iron is utilized for soldering small joint terminals. A poker is used for making pilot holes for fixing wood screws. Lastly, a line tester is used for testing the current.

PRECAUTIONS:

  • Ensure that the insulation of wire reaches up to accessory
  • Do not over tighten the screw
  • Ensure that the base wire is not touching any part of accessory.
