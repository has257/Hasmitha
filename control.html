Control Wiring

Control wiring is the wiring used for controlling electrical circuits and systems in a home. This type of wiring is crucial for managing lighting systems, HVAC, security systems, and more.

Components:

  • Control panels: Houses relays,contractors,timers and other control devices.
  • Timers and sensors: Devices that provide time-based or sensor-based control.
  • Fuses and circuit breakers: For protecting control circuits.
  • Actuators: Devices that convert electrical signals into mechanical movement.

Tools:

  • Wire strippers and cutters.
  • Screwdrivers and pilers.
  • Multimeter.
  • Crimping Tool.
  • Label maker
