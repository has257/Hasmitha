Power Wiring

Power wiring is the electrical wiring used to distribute electrical power throughout a home. It is crucial for running high-power appliances and ensuring a stable power supply.

Components:

  • Power Supply: Typically, an AC supply from the mains, often 230 V or 110V depending on the country.
  • Sockets and Plugs: For connecting electrical appliances.
  • Wires and Cables: Conduct electricity from the power supply to various outlets.
  • Switches: To control the flow of electricity.
  • Protection Devices: Circuit breakers, fuses, and residual current devices(RCDs) for safety.

Tools:

  • Wire Strippers and Cutters.
  • Screwdrivers and Pilers.
  • Multimeter and Insulation resistance tester.
  • Conduit bender(if using metal conduits).
