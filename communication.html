Communication Wiring

Communication Wiring involves setting up the infrastructure for transmitting data, voice, and video signals within a building or campus.

Components:

  • Cables: Category 5e, category 6, fibre optic cables for data transmisssion.
  • Connectors: RJ45 connectors for cat 5e/cat 6 cables;various connectors for fibre optic cables.
  • Switches and Routers: For network management and distribution.
  • Network Interface Cards(NICs): Installed in computers and devices to connect to the network

Tools:

  • Cable tester.
  • Crimping Tool.
  • Wire Strippers.
  • Punch-Down Tool.
  • Fish Tape(for pulling cables through conduits).
